EXAM HALL SEAT ALLOCATION SYSTEM

The purpose of developing exam hall seating arrangement system is to computerized the traditional way of conducting exams and help staffs in allocating exam hall easily without any burden. Another purpose of developing this software is to generate the report automatically during exams at the end of the session or in between the session.
