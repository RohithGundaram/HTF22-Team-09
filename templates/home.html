EXAM HALL SEAT ALLOCATION SYSTEM

The purpose of developing exam hall seating arrangement system is to computerized the traditional way of conducting exams and help staffs in allocating exam hall easily without any burden. This website also helps in developing software which generates the report automatically during exams at the end of the session or in between the session.

This website guides the student to the wing , floor , room and seat alloted very clearly so the student doesn't have to face any seating issues on the exam day. The proper seating arrangement is designed so that no student beloging to the same section is seated together .
